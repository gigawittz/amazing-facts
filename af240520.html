4 Facts You Should Know About TikTok

1-In 2016, TikTok started as a Chinese project called Douyin, which is still the Chinese name of the app.
2-According to the latest studies, more than 800 million people worldwide are actively using TikTok. That’s already way more than Twitter, Linkedin, and Snapchat.
3-The average TikTok user spends more than 50 minutes per day on the app.
4-The ratings of the app is down to 1.3 stars from 4.5 stars a few days back. TikTok received as many as 4 million new comments on Google Play Store — with most of them being negative.
