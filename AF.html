Tribute to our Hockey legend

Balbir Singh Sr.

May 25, 2020 by Gourav Sharma

The iconic hockey player died at Fortis Hospital Mohali, he was 95 and was fighting with bronchial pneumonia and later developed a blood clot in his head.


The following are some facts about the legend

  1. He was the only Indian among 16 legends chosen by the International committee across modern Olympic history
  2. His world record for most goals scored by an individual in Men's hockey final is still unbeaten (In the 1952 Olympic final against Holland, India thrashed the opposition 6-1, with Balbir Singh Sr scoring a whopping five goals.)
  3. A former India captain, Singh was part of teams that won three Olympic gold medals (1948, 1952, 1956) and as Asian Games silver (1958).

He will be always remembered for his contribution to Indian Hockey,
May his soul Rest in Peace
